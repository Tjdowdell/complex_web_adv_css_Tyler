Welcome to AllStyle Homes - FAQ

Where do I start if I want to build a new home?

A good start is to arrange a no obligation consultation with the AllStyle Homes building team. Your builder will be able to take you through finance, house and land packages and price

Am I able to make changes to floor plans for the house?

Of course. After we have finished drawing up the first floor plan, our team lets the customer do a review of the plan and make any changes that the customer sees fit to suit their lifestyle

During the building process, can I access the worksite to see how the house is coming along?

Only with the permission of the builder due to health and safety requirements. You cannot walk through the site unsupervised during the construction phase of your new home.
